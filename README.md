## Digital_Literacy_VITyarthi_Project
This Digital Literacy Portfolio, created as Student Digital Ambassador, guides peers through the modern digital world. It covers professional branding via GitHub, infographic design in Prezi, safe internet practices, various coding and collaborative platforms and email etiquette to foster a secure, professional college environment.

**Summary of modules :**

In Module 1, students are taught dynamic visual communication skills through hands-on training with presentation software. I became proficient with presentation software such as Prezi, Canva, and Miro, learning that digital literacy starts with the skill of breaking down complicated information into "cool" text and images. This experience laid the groundwork for Module 2, where students are taught professional identity skills. Through platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Stack Overflow, I learned to create a holistic digital presence that highlights my technical skills to a worldwide network while promoting my academic contributions.

In Module 3, my technical utility skills were refined through learning platforms that facilitate real-time collaboration. I became familiar with Google Workspace, which offers real-time collaboration capabilities, as well as coding platforms such as HackerRank and CodeChef, where I solved challenging coding problems to earn badges that verify my logic skills.

The final stages of the course, Modules 4 and 5, dealt with the human and safety sides of technology. I perfected my Email Etiquette to suit professional needs, as well as learning about Cybercrime Prevention, which ranges from financial fraud detection to understanding crime flowcharts, to ensure that my technological advancement goes hand-in-hand with my dedication to security.
